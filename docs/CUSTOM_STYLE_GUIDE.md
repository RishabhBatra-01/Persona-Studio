# üé® Custom Style Feature - User Guide

## What is Custom Style?

The **Custom Style** option allows you to create completely unique headshots by describing your own vision. Instead of choosing from preset styles, you can describe exactly what you want!

---

## How to Use Custom Style

### Step 1: Upload Your Photo
1. Upload a casual selfie or portrait photo
2. Wait for it to process

### Step 2: Select "Custom Style"
1. Scroll through the style options
2. Click on the **"Custom Style"** card (pink icon with a plus sign)
3. A text input box will appear

### Step 3: Describe Your Vision
Type a detailed description of what you want. The more specific, the better!

**Example descriptions:**
- `futuristic sci-fi portrait with neon lights and cyberpunk aesthetic`
- `vintage 1920s style with art deco background and elegant attire`
- `outdoor mountain setting with natural lighting and hiking gear`
- `minimalist white background with soft studio lighting`
- `cozy coffee shop vibe with warm lighting and casual sweater`
- `professional medical setting with white coat and modern clinic background`
- `tech startup office with exposed brick walls and casual business attire`

### Step 4: Generate
1. Click the **"Create Magic"** button
2. Wait 5-15 seconds for AI to generate your custom headshot
3. Review the result!

## How It Works

1. **AI Analysis**: The AI analyzes your uploaded photo to understand your facial features
2. **Style Application**: It applies your custom description while preserving your identity
3. **Smart Generation**: The AI interprets your text and creates a professional headshot matching your vision
4. **Identity Preservation**: Your facial features, bone structure, and unique characteristics remain unchanged

---

## Refining Your Result

If the first result isn't perfect:

1. **Use the Edit Feature**: Type refinements like:
   - "make background brighter"
   - "add glasses"
   - "warmer lighting"
   - "more professional attire"

2. **Try Again**: Click "New" and adjust your custom prompt with more specific details

3. **Be Specific**: Instead of "nice background", try "modern minimalist office with plants"

## Common Questions

**Q: How long should my description be?**
A: 10-30 words is ideal. Be specific but concise.

**Q: Can I change my face shape or features?**
A: No, the AI preserves your facial identity. It only changes styling, background, lighting, and attire.

**Q: Can I combine multiple styles?**
A: Yes! For example: "corporate professional meets creative artist with modern office and colorful accents"

**Q: What if I don't like the result?**
A: Use the edit feature to refine it, or start over with a more specific description.

**Q: Does it work with any photo?**
A: Best results come from clear, well-lit photos where your face is visible and not obscured.

## Troubleshooting

**Problem**: Result doesn't match my description
- **Solution**: Be more specific. Add details about lighting, background, and attire.

**Problem**: Can't type in the text box
- **Solution**: Make sure you've clicked the Custom Style card first. The text box only appears when selected.

**Problem**: "Create Magic" button is disabled
- **Solution**: You need to type at least a few words in the custom description box.

**Problem**: Result looks too different from my photo
- **Solution**: The AI preserves your face but changes everything else. If you want minimal changes, use preset styles instead.
