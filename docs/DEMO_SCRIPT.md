## üé§ PRESENTATION TIPS

### Voice & Delivery:
- Speak at a moderate pace (not too fast, not too slow)
- Vary your tone to maintain interest
- Pause after important points
- Use "power words": instantly, transform, professional, simple, powerful
- Avoid filler words: um, uh, like, you know

### Body Language (if in person):
- Stand confidently, shoulders back
- Use open hand gestures
- Make eye contact with different audience members
- Smile when appropriate
- Move purposefully (don't pace nervously)

### Screen Presence (if remote):
- Look at camera, not screen
- Ensure good lighting on your face
- Minimize background distractions
- Use a quality microphone
- Test screen sharing beforehand
